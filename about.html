About Us

Our aims are promoting original research works in the fields of Basic and Applied Sciences, Health Sciences, Humanities, Social Sciences, Agriculture Sciences, Engineering and Technology. The qualified academic professors, scientists, young scientists and post-doctoral researchers from different reputed institutions and research stations were selected as editorial board members.

IJRIAR peer-reviewed, open access online International Journal. The Journal do not charge any article submission fees. As the International Journal of Recent Innovations in Academic Research (IJRIAR) does not get outside funding so authors are expected to pay a minimum amount of cost towards article processing charges.

About Editor-in-Chief
Prof. Paul J. Udo
B.Sc.(Hons.) Fisheries Biology, PhD (Aquaculture) Calabar

Paul is a specialist in Aquaculture and highly skilled in the development of fish culture designs as well as in their construction with great interest in the building of elaborate and complicated /large farm structures. He is a beneficiary of several scholarly awards, scholarships and fellowships some of which are:

  • GKSS-Forschungzentrum fellowship
  • University of Calabar post graduate fellowship
  • Government of Cross River State undergraduate scholarship and
  • Common wealth fellowship

Prof. Paul Udo is an aquaculture consultant and is highly experienced in the development of aquaculture structures especially in difficult terrains. He has published several articles in national and international reputed journals. He has been attended and presented several scientific papers in national and international seminars and conferences/workshops.

Area(s) of Specialization

  • Aquaculture techniques including designs and construction of enclosures
  • Fish  Nutrition
  • Shrimp  and Catfish production in unconventional culture systems
  • Fish physiology
  • Aquatic ecology
  • Fisheries Managements

Current Research

  • Aquaculture nutrition with special references to the development of standardized feeds for cultivable species using local raw materials.
  • Culture of freshwater Prawns.
  • Development of Aquaculture structures with particular references to difficult terrains.
  • Determination of the food values of edible indigenous aquatic species.

Professional Affiliations(s)

  • Fisheries Society of Nigeria (FISON)
  • Network of Tropical Aquaculture Scientist(NTAS)
