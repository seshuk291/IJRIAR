Reviewers Responsibilities

Confidentiality-During Review Process

Reviewers are solely responsible for the submitted manuscripts for review and those are considered as confidential files and should not discussed with others, except with the Editorial team or Editorial staff members until review process completed.

Standards of Objectivity

During review process the Reviewers should conduct reviews objectively. Personal criticism of the author is unprofessional or inappropriate.

Fast-Track Reviews

As our Journal publishing articles on monthly basis the reviewers are requested to finalize the review process in stipulated time provided. If the reviewer is busy with his academic activities and any other commitments please let us know immediately and such time refuse the invitation for review process.

Promptness

Any reviewer who believe that he/she is not qualified to review the received research work should inform the Editor-in-Chief promptly.
