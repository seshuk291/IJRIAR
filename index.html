Welcome to IJRIAR

International Journal of Recent Innovations in Academic Research (IJRIAR) is an open access, peer-reviewed, multidisciplinary International Journal, that provides rapid publication (Monthly) of research articles, review articles and short communications in all subjects: Basic and Applied Sciences, Health Sciences, Humanities, Social Sciences, Agriculture Sciences and also Engineering and Technology.
